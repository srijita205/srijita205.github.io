About me

From rock layers
to data layers.

I hold an MSc in Geology from Central University of Punjab and an MBA specialising in Data Science & Business Analytics from Bengal Institute of Business Studies, Kolkata. It's an unusual path — and that's exactly the point.

"A geologist reads strata to understand history. A data analyst reads layers to shape the future."

Geology trained me in pattern recognition, structured anomaly detection, and thinking clearly under ambiguity — the exact skills that make a great analyst. I pivoted deliberately, certified with IIT Kanpur and IBM, built a project portfolio across FMCG, retail, e-commerce and automation — and I work at the edge of where traditional analytics meets agentic AI tools, using AI as a force multiplier, not a crutch.

Currently targeting Data Analyst, BI Analyst, and Business Analyst roles — I bring a cross-domain perspective, a builder's mindset, and the kind of analytical rigour that geology trained into me before data science ever did.
